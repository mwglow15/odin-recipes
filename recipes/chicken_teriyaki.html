Chicken Teriyaki

Description

Chicken Teriyaki is a dish made fomr juicy chicken thighs, seared then tossed in a sweet, gingery sauce. Served with white rice and sauteed veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds chicken thighs
  • 4 Tbs Sake, divided
  • 1 Tbs Cornstarch
  • 2 Tbs Grapeseed Oil
  • 1 2in piece of ginger, minced or grated
  • 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 Cup Mirin
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • Green Onion

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken thighs into 1 inch pieces and place in a medium mixing bowl
  2. Mix the cornstarch with 2 Tbs of Sake in a small bowl until fully combined
  3. Pour the cornstarch slurry on the chicken and toss to coat
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the Ginger, Soy Sauce, Mirin, and remaining Sake
  5. Pour 1 Tbs of the oil into a fry pan and heat until barely smoking
  6. Add half of the chicken to the pan in a single layer
  7. Cook undisturbed until the bottom forms a crispy, golden brown crust. About 2 minutes
  8. Turn the chicken to cook the other side, About 2 minutes
  9. Remove cooked chicken from the pan and place in a clean bowl. Repeat with the rest of the chicken
  10. In the same pan, reduce to medium and pour in the soy sauce mixture
  11. Stir mixture, scraping up the browned bits, until it has thickedned to where a dragged spoon leaves a trail
  12. Pour in the cooked chicken and accumulated juices and top with the pepper
  13. Stire to coat the chicken and continue cooking until the sauce clings to the chicken
  14. Remove from heat
  15. Serve with white rice and top with sliced green onion
