Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is made from breaded and fried chicken breast, topped with pasta sauce and mozzarella cheese. It is then served with spaghetti and garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 3 Large Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 Cup Whole Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/2 Cup Panko Breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 Cup Seasoned Breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 Cup Olive Oil
  • Pasta Sauce
  • 8 oz ShreddedMozzarella Cheese
  • Grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325f and pour olive oil in a fry pan to give a depth of ~1/4in and heat until a small drop of water crackles when dropped in the oil
  2. Cut the chicken breasts in half to make 6 thinner breasts
  3. Whisk together the milk and egg in a bowl
  4. Mix panko breadcrumbs and breadcrumbs on a plate
  5. Coat each chicken breast in the egg wash then immediatly coat with the breadcrumb mixture
  6. 2 at a time, place the breaded chicken breasts in the hot oil. When it becomes a golden brown turn it over. ~2-3 minutes per side. When both sides have been fried, remove from the oil and place on a paper lined plate
  7. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce on the bottome of a 13x9 Pyrex baking dish
  8. Place the breaded chicken breasts on the pasta sauce layer well spaced from eachother
  9. Top each breast with pasta sauce and shredded mozzarella
  10. Place in the oven and cook for 12-14 minutes
  11. Serve with Spaghetti, Garlic Bread, and Grated Parmesan
